# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, colour,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while accepting correction.
- Focus criticism on ideas and changes, not people.
- Respect privacy and do not publish confidential information.
- Disclose conflicts of interest and material AI assistance where relevant.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, and sustained disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact
method on their GitHub profile. Maintainers may edit or remove content and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behaviour threatens the
safety or health of the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially
as reasonably possible.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone officially represents
the project elsewhere. Enforcement decisions should consider context, impact,
remediation, and the safety of affected participants.
